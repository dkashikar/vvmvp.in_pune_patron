Introduction

Ved Vignan Mahavidyapeeth (VVMVP) operates multiple ashrams and spiritual centers across India, offering courses in meditation, yoga, Vedic studies & community service. As a Patron, your contribution empowers us to maintain our campuses, support Gurukul education, and continue our seva mission.

Choose an Ashram

Why Become a Patron?

  • Preserve our Vedic heritage and Gurukul tradition
  • Support spiritual courses, satsangs, and retreats
  • Enable community outreach and natural harmony initiatives
  • Uphold maintenance of ashram infrastructures and goshala

What Your Patronage Supports

  • Campus upkeep & hygiene
  • Educational infrastructure & scholarships
  • Festivals, homas, satsangs
  • Food programs, health camps & seva projects

How to Join

  1. Select your preferred ashram above
  2. Make an annual contribution (₹10,000 or as per location)
  3. Choose your payment method and complete the form on the ashram page
  4. Receive acknowledgment, Patron ID card, 80G tax receipt
